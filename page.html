Facts about dogs

Dogs are loyal and intelligent animals, known for their strong bond with humans. They come in various breeds, each with unique traits and characteristics, from playful Retrievers to protective German Shepherds. Dogs have an incredible sense of smell, up to 100,000 times more sensitive than humans, making them valuable in search-and-rescue and detection work.
